Welcome To CvSU Silang Campus

Welcome to the official site of Cavite State University, Silang Campus.
Here, we will give you the information and guidelines of the university. To help the new students/upcoming students to be informed what the university has to offer. This website will provide all information you'll need.

Vision

The premier University in historic Cavite recognized for excellence in the development of globally competitive and morally upright individuals.

Mission

Cavite State University shall provide excellent, equitable and relevant educational oppotunities in the arts, sciences, and technology through quality instructions and reponsive research and development activities. It shall produce professional, skilled, and morally upright individuals for globally competitiveness.

Quality Policy

We Commit to the highest standards of education, Value our stakeholders, Strive for continual improvement of our product and services, and Uphold the University's tenets of Truth, Excellence and Service to produce globally competitive and morally upright individual.

History

Cavite State University - Silang (CvSU-Silang) is a public university in Silang, Cavite and one of the campuses of Cavite State University (CvSU), an educational institution founded in 1906 as the Indang Intermediate School.
